Services

What We Do.

Upgrading the current voting system so that one can cast a vote

Problem

There are many reasons why individuals don’t vote. Reasons include but are not limited to the time taken during the registration process. Not only is it very time consuming but physically straining as well. Being influenced by other people such as friends or family also makes individuals unsure of their stance and hence, they hesitate to vote. We also see that during the results, corrupt officials can tamper with the votes and hence make a huge impact on the results.

Solution

We have come up with a unique solution to upgrade/ this voting system and hence help improve governance as a whole. We are suggesting a decentralized E-Voting platform with pre-existing emotion authentication. As the data in a Decentralized system is validated, it will make the voting data more secure and tamper-free.

Dataset

We have used pretty famous original FER-2013(fer2013) dataset to train our “Emotion recognition classifier” from facial expressions. To train the gender analysis model we have used IMDB datasets, containing raw images and the metadata with the cropped faces (40% margin).

Data Analysis

We have used Keras CNN model and openCV and were able to achieve 96% test accuracy in gender classification on IMDB dataset and 66% test accuracy for emotion classification on fer2013 emotion dataset.
